Item card AI menu — design concepts

Each listing concept keeps two AI blocks: Titel and Beschreibung. Design 11 combines Listing Studio (#5) with the Product Card generator and photo-add controls under the item image.

Design 11 · NEW
Listing Studio + Product Card
#5 modal + card generator tab. Under the item photo: Upload, Camera, Import URL, gallery thumbs → feed card generation.
Design 01
Split Studio
Two equal AI blocks side-by-side under a slim item header. Title left, description right. Primary actions only: Generate, Copy, Apply.
Design 02
Row Inline Expand
Inventory row stays; expanding opens two stacked AI fields inline. Removes Parse/Price/Kleinanzeigen clutter from the AI menu.
Design 03
Slide Drawer
Right drawer with Title block on top and Description below. Sticky Generate for both. Actions reduced to Edit / Sold / Delete outside.
Design 04
Popover Duo Cards
AI button opens a compact popover with two mini-cards (Titel / Beschreibung). One Generate All, per-block Copy.
Design 05
Listing Studio Modal
Full modal “Listing Studio”: left preview of item, right two AI editors. Owner-only price hints strip at top.
Design 06
Twin Editors Panel
Wide panel: Title (80-char meter) + Description textarea as equal twin editors with shared Generate Listing.
Design 07
Accordion Blocks
Under the item: two accordion sections AI Titel and AI Beschreibung. Collapsed by default; Generate inside each.
Design 08
Bottom Sheet Dual
Mobile-first bottom sheet with Title and Description as two swipeable or stacked blocks. Minimal chrome.
Design 09
Operator Console
Dense ops UI: flags stay sparse; AI opens a console with TITLE / DESCRIPTION panes and char counters. No redundant copy variants.
Design 10
Action Rail + Panels
Left icon rail (photos, card, sold). Right expands into Title panel + Description panel. Clearest separation of AI vs logistics.
